Foreign nurses in nursing homes face language barriers & manpower crunch

Singapore faces manpower shortage in nursing homes, and up to 85% of the care staff are foreign workers who face language barriers with local residents. To understand workflows, I interviewed experts and connected with Kwong Wai Shiu Hospital's innovation team, which linked me with the nurses.

I began my shadowing sessions at Kwong Wai Shiu and observed that nurses spend a large portion of their time at their workstations, overwhelmed with heaps of manual and time-consuming documentation.

Interestingly enough, nurses have good ideas to save time and manage hectic workflows. Some examples include: placing reminders near residents' headboards, efficient hacked-together trolleys and matching labels on personal-use items.
