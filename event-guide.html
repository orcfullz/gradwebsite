By car
From Ayer Rajah Expressway (AYE), take the Normanton Park or South Buona Vista Road exit and then take the turn into Science Park Drive.
By train
Take the Circle Line and alight at Kent Ridge MRT and then take Exit B. Visitors can access the park by foot or take the Singapore Science Park shuttle bus.
By bus
Take public Bus No. 92/ 92A from Buona Vista MRT Station. Other public Bus services plying along Buona Vista Flyover are 14, 33, 97, 166, 197, 963 and 963e.
